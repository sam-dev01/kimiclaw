Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

KimiClaw AI ("we", "us", or "our") operates kimiclaw.in. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Contact information (name, email address) submitted via our contact form
  • Usage data collected automatically via Google Analytics (pages visited, time on site, device type)
  • Chat data collected via Tawk.to live chat if you initiate a conversation
  • IP address and browser information for security and analytics purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your project enquiries and provide our services
  • To improve our website and user experience
  • To understand how visitors interact with our site via analytics
  • To communicate project updates and relevant information

3. Cookies and Tracking

We use the following cookies and tracking tools:

  • Google Analytics (GA4) — analytics cookies to understand website traffic. You can opt out via Google's opt-out browser add-on.
  • Tawk.to — live chat functionality. Their privacy policy is available at tawk.to/privacy-policy.
  • Session cookies — necessary for the website to function correctly.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data with:

  • Google Analytics (analytics processing)
  • Tawk.to (live chat service)
  • Our hosting provider for website operation

5. Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained for up to 2 years for business correspondence purposes. Analytics data is retained per Google Analytics default settings (26 months).

6. Your Rights (GDPR / UK GDPR)

If you are located in the EU or UK, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Request restriction of processing
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@kimiclaw.in.

7. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have the right to request disclosure of personal information collected, to request deletion of personal information, and to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your own, including the United States and India. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

9. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, secure session handling, and regular security reviews to protect your information.

10. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The updated date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
