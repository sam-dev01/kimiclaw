## Article rules

Every post should include:

- one clear target keyword
- one supporting keyword group
- a specific audience
- a useful introduction without fluff
- examples, not generic AI text
- one internal link to `/blog`
- two internal links to related posts
- one service CTA near the middle or end
- author attribution
- a strong meta title and meta description

## Monetization rule

Do not apply for ad monetization yet.

Apply later after:

- at least 20 strong posts are live
- traffic is consistent
- the blog has original depth
- article pages look trustworthy and complete
